## 0. The Narrative

**Problem:** Every year, more than 180,000 people sit the Australian citizenship test, and roughly one in three fail at least once. The questions are drawn from a dense government booklet, *Our Common Bond*, available in 40 community languages — but the test itself is only in English. Most migrants prepare with outdated quiz apps or static PDFs that do not adapt to their language level, test date, or weak areas, creating avoidable anxiety, repeat attempts, and extra cost.

**Insight:** Citizenship prep is not a classic "recurring SaaS" use case: it's a short, high‑stakes, 3–6 month lifecycle tied to a specific exam date. The market self‑renews each year as new migrants become eligible, but each individual only needs intense support for a limited period. **Pricing, engagement, and growth must be designed around the exam cycle, not endless subscriptions.**

**Solution:** **CitizenMate** — an AI‑powered, multilingual web app with killer mock tests, bilingual study mode, test‑date‑anchored study plans, calibrated readiness scores, and lifecycle‑aware engagement — all designed to reduce anxiety and maximise pass probability within the user's specific exam window.

**Why now:** No dominant SaaS player exists. Existing tools are static quiz apps with poor UX, English‑only content, no AI, and no lifecycle‑aware design. The moat comes from multilingual content aligned with official materials, calibrated readiness data, curated trust‑based partnerships, and a product explicitly designed around the real exam lifecycle.

**Why "CitizenMate":** The name embodies the Australian spirit of mateship — the idea that you don't face challenges alone. CitizenMate is your study partner, your guide, your mate for the citizenship journey. It's warm, trustworthy, and unmistakably Australian.

### 2.2 Critical Market Insight — Short Lifecycle, Not Recurring

> [!WARNING]
> **Validated finding:** The citizenship test prep audience is a **fast-turnover cohort**, not a traditional recurring subscription base. Most users have a 3–6 month study window. LTV depends on capturing value within that window — not multi-year retention.

**Implications for CitizenMate's business model:**
- The **60‑day Exam Sprint Pass is the primary CTA** — not a monthly subscription. Subscriptions exist for slower learners and institutional buyers.
- Additional revenue from **micro-transactions** (extra mock tests, cram mode, AI unlocks) for price-sensitive users
- The user base **self-renews annually** (180K+ new test-takers/year), offsetting individual churn
- Engagement mechanics must anchor to **test date countdown**, not open-ended streaks

## 10. Engagement Design — Lifecycle-Aware

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Key insight:** Generic gamification (streaks, XP, leaderboards) is less effective for adult learners under high emotional and time pressure. CitizenMate's engagement system is anchored to the **test date** and designed to **reduce anxiety**, not just increase time-in-app.

### Onboarding Flow

```
Step 1: "G'day! When is your citizenship test?"
   → Enter date or "I haven't booked yet"

Step 2: "How comfortable are you with English?"
   → Beginner / Intermediate / Confident

Step 3: "What language would you like study support in?"
   → Select from available languages

Step 4: "Let's see where you stand, mate"
   → Diagnostic mini-test (10 questions across all topics)

Step 5: Immediate results + personalised timeline
   → "Your test is in 47 days. Here's your plan to be ready."
   → Show topic mastery gaps as clear upgrade moment
```

### Core Engagement Loop

| Element | Design | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Test-date countdown** | Always visible; drives urgency | Anchors all activity to the real outcome |
| **Daily confidence check-in** | 3–5 questions + adjusted plan | Makes users feel guided, not alone |
| **Topic mastery dashboard** | Visual progress per section | Clear finish line for each area |
| **"You're ready" threshold** | Mock test series → completion certificate | Anxiety-reducing; qualifies for pass guarantee |
| **Lapsed-user nudges** | Push/email based on test-date proximity | "Your test is in 12 days — you haven't practiced in 5 days, mate" |
| **Progress milestones** | "You've mastered Australian Values!" | Celebration tied to real readiness, not arbitrary XP |

### Anxiety-Reducing Framing

Instead of:
- ❌ "You failed this mock test"
- ❌ "Your score: 11/20"
- ❌ "You need to study more"

CitizenMate says:
- ✅ "You've already mastered 65% of what you need, mate"
- ✅ "Focus on these 3 topics and you'll be ready"
- ✅ "CitizenMate users who reached this point were 89% likely to pass"

### 14.1 PWA Implementation Plan

CitizenMate ships as a **Progressive Web App** — users can install it to their home screen and use it like a native app without an app store download. This is critical for the migrant audience who may have limited device storage or be unfamiliar with app stores.

| PWA Feature | Implementation | Phase |
|---|---|---|
| **Web App Manifest** | `manifest.json` with app name, icons (192px + 512px), theme colour (#FFB347), splash screen, `display: standalone` | Phase 1 (MVP) |
| **Service Worker** | `@serwist/next` for automatic caching of static assets, study content, and question bank | Phase 1 (MVP) |
| **Install Prompt** | Custom "Add CitizenMate to your phone" banner after first mock test completion (deliberate timing, not on first visit) | Phase 1 (MVP) |
| **Offline Mock Tests** | Cache question bank + rationales locally so users can practice without internet | Phase 2 |
| **Push Notifications** | Test-date countdown reminders, lapsed-user nudges, readiness milestones via Web Push API | Phase 2 |
| **Background Sync** | Sync quiz results and progress data when connectivity returns | Phase 2–3 |
| **Periodic Sync** | Pre-fetch new content and updated question bank in the background | Phase 3 |

> [!TIP]
> **Why PWA over native apps:** The target audience (migrants preparing for citizenship) often share devices, have limited storage, and need immediate access. PWA eliminates app-store friction, works cross-platform (iOS + Android + desktop), and lets CitizenMate ship updates instantly without app review delays. Native apps are deferred to Phase 4 as a premium add-on once the web product is validated.
